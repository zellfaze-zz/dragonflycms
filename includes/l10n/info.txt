
Languages are constructed according to RFC 1766 ([ISO 639]-[ISO 3166]).
This means: "[language code]-[country code]".
Country code is not required.
Lists of all codes:
	http://www.loc.gov/standards/iso639-2/php/code_list.php
	http://www.iso.org/iso/home/standards/country_codes/country_names_and_code_elements.htm
